About

About

HUMAN SIMULATE is an indie game circle that creates story-driven games centered on kindness, bittersweet emotions, and the changing distance between people.

Our works focus on romance adventures and character-driven stories, carefully portraying the time spent with characters and the relationships that gradually change through daily choices.

Rather than relying only on dramatic events, we aim to express warmth and emotional nuance found in small conversations and everyday interactions.

What matters in our work

  • Gentle emotional shifts born through conversation and quiet moments
  • Relationships that change little by little through everyday choices
  • Stories that value warmth and pacing over spectacle

Streaming Guidelines

Streaming Guidelines

We welcome gameplay videos, livestreams, and videos with commentary or impressions. Please review the following guidelines before posting or streaming.

English

Gameplay videos, livestreams, clips, thumbnails, and monetized content are allowed as long as they follow the guidelines below.

Request

Please include a link to the Steam store page of the game in your video description or stream description.

Please refrain from

  • Uploads or streams with no commentary, impressions, or added value, such as silent and unedited raw playthroughs
  • Redistributing in-game assets on their own, including character art, voices, BGM, or event CGs
  • Sharing extracted data, analysis results, or modified game data
  • Showing major spoilers directly in thumbnails or titles

About music

In-game BGM may be used only as part of gameplay videos or streams.

Please do not upload or distribute the BGM by itself.

Other

  • Please follow the rules of each streaming or video platform.
  • These guidelines may be updated without prior notice.
